Task 2: Business Card Design

Objective: Design a professional and creative business card for personal or brand use
Tools Used: Canva / Photoshop / Illustrator
Skills Applied: Branding, layout, typography, color schemes, professional aesthetics

Description:
The business card is designed to reflect a clean, professional look while showcasing creativity. Focus was given to readability, visual balance, and brand identity.

Learning Outcomes:
Gained experience in creating business-ready graphics
Learned the importance of minimalism and clarity in professional design
Developed skills in aligning visual identity with brand representation
